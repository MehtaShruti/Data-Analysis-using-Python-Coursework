Debate continues over government's actions in imposing quarantines upon American citizens during Ebola outbreak, even as epidemic and attendant panic has largely subsided; state governments in several cases exceeded CDC recommendations to monitor those who may have come in contact with Ebola, raising questions over legality of their actions; some of those confined describe lost wages, stress and lack of access to services and support.
